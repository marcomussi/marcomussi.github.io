About Me

Marco Mussi is a PhD candidate in Information Technology at the Department of Electronics, Information and Bioengineering of Politecnico di Milano. He received his Master's degree in Computer Science and Engineering at Politecnico di Milano in 2019. After a period as a research fellow in the AIRLab research team, he started the PhD in collaboration with ML cube. His main research topics revolve around artificial intelligence and machine learning, focusing on reinforcement learning applied to advertising. He contributed to several industrial research projects funded by both private and public Italian companies.
