I am a Postdoctoral Researcher with the Dipartimento di Elettronica, Informazione e Bioingegneria, in the Artificial Intelligence and Robotic Laboratory of Politecnico di Milano. I received the Doctor of Philosophy in Information Technology (with honors) at Politecnico di Milano in June 2024, supervised by Professor Marcello Restelli. My main research topics revolve around artificial intelligence and machine learning, focusing on online learning and reinforcement learning both from theoretical and applicative perspectives. I have contributed to several industrial research projects funded by both private and public Italian companies.
